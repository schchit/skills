**# System Logic: The 5-Dimension Calculus**

#### **Step 1: Strategic Entity Triage (身份分诊)**
* Identify the bid's nature: **Military (军采)** vs. **Gov (政采)**.
* Scan for "Exclusionary Rules": Does it require specialized military licenses (Entity A) or is it an SME-exclusive bid (Entity B)?
* **Decision Verdict:** Choose the "Lead Bidder" from `profiles.md` and justify the choice based on policy bonuses or qualification moats.

#### **Step 2: Compliance & "Poison Pill" Audit (红线扫描)**
* Search for "Mandatory Requirements" (资格性审查).
* Identify "Poison Pills": Hidden clauses (e.g., CMMI5, local tax records for 3+ years, or specific legacy system experience) that could lead to immediate disqualification.

#### **Step 3: Quantitative Scoring - The Hard Gap (商务/业绩损耗)**
* Analyze the "Performance Scoring" (业绩评分) table.
* Compare the chosen entity's history (from `profiles.md`) with the max score criteria (e.g., 10M+ single contract vs. 1M+).
* **Calculus:** Determine the exact point deficit (e.g., "Losing 4 points due to lack of 50M cumulative revenue").

#### **Step 4: Qualitative Scoring - The Soft Premium (技术/人员溢价)**
* Evaluate how our **LLM Agent/Simulation/UAV Cluster** tech (our "Tech Edge") can secure a "Class-A" score in the Technical Proposal.
* Check if our **PhD/Senior Title density** can max out the Personnel scores.
* **Calculus:** Can the "Soft Premium" offset the "Hard Gap"?

#### **Step 5: Pricing Strategy - The Delta Model (价格对冲)**
* For **Entity B**, apply the **10%-20% SME price deduction** if the bid is a Government Procurement.
* **Math Model:** Calculate the "Winning Price Threshold" (中标价阈值). If we lose $n$ points in Business/Tech, calculate the required price drop to recover those $n$ points based on the price weight.
