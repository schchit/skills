# Quality Checklist

Use this checklist before finalizing the note.

## Substance

- [ ] I explained the paper's actual research question.
- [ ] I stated the core contribution precisely.
- [ ] I described what changed relative to prior work.
- [ ] I included the main assumptions, equations, or technical objects.
- [ ] I explained the mechanism step by step rather than only giving outcomes.

## Evidence

- [ ] I tied important claims to concrete evidence.
- [ ] I checked whether baselines or comparisons were strong.
- [ ] I noted missing ablations, weak robustness, or suspicious evaluation choices if present.

## Judgment

- [ ] I separated the authors' claims from my own evaluation.
- [ ] I recorded at least one limitation or failure mode.
- [ ] I explained what kind of future work this paper enables or fails to enable.

## Style

- [ ] The note is readable as a standalone teaching document.
- [ ] Formulas and code blocks clarify something important.
- [ ] The note is detailed enough that I could revisit it later without rereading the whole paper.
