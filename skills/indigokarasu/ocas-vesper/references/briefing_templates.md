# Vesper Briefing Templates

## Morning Structure
Greeting → weather (inline, no header) → optional sections: 📅 Today, ✉️ Messages, 🧭 Logistics, 📈 Markets, 🔐 Decisions, 🛠 System.

## Evening Structure
Greeting → optional sections: ✉️ Messages, 📈 Markets, 🧭 Logistics, 🔐 Decisions, 🛠 System. No weather. No summaries of attended meetings.

## Weather
Morning only. Inline in opening paragraph. Example: "Today in San Francisco it will be sunny with a high around 72°F." Multi-location for travel days.

## Opportunities
Surface the opportunity in natural language. Never expose the analysis. Example: "There's an opening at Gloss Nail Spa at 4:00 PM next Wednesday and that time is free on your calendar. Would you like me to book it?"

## Decisions
Include: option, benefit, cost. Frame as optional. Example: "I found a tool called Granola that converts meeting recordings into structured notes. It costs $18/month. If you'd like I can set it up."
